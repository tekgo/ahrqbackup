Title

Stable coronary artery disease: percentage of patients with documentation in the medical record of prognostic assessment preceding or following a course of pharmacologic therapy.

Brief Abstract

Description

This measure is used to assess the percentage of patients age 18 years and older with documentation in the medical record of prognostic assessment preceding or following a course of pharmacologic therapy.

Rationale

The priority aim addressed by this measure is to increase appropriate clinical risk assessment and stress imaging for stable coronary artery disease patients to determine risk stratification prior to decisions on medical therapy and revascularization.

Prognostic testing is appropriate for patients in whom risk determination remains unclear after initial evaluations have been completed, or in whom cardiac catheterization is deemed inappropriate by the cardiologist. Prognostic testing may precede or follow an initial course of pharmacological therapy.
